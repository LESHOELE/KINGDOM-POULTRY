WELCOME TO KINGDOM POULTRY

BACKGROUND

Kingdom poultry is a locally owned agribusiness established in Maseru with the vision of becoming a leading supplier of high-quality poultry products in the Kingdom of Lesotho. The business was founded in response to the growing demand for fresh,locally produced chicken and eggs. As Lesotho heavily rely on puoltry imported from South Africa. It is a partnership business formed by 10 partners in 2022. The businss main offices are located in Maseru but it operates in all Lesotho districts.The company operates using modern poultry management, ensuring proper feeding and vaccinating
